🐐 LYRIC GOAT

Mumble → Lyrics. Your flow. Your words.

🎙
Recording setup
Use headphones or find a quiet room. Get close to your mic — 6 to 12 inches. Background noise confuses the transcription and kills syllable accuracy.
🎵
How to mumble
Mumble WITH rhythm, not just words. The flow and cadence matter more than pronouncing words clearly. Natural pauses between phrases = natural bar breaks. Think in bars, not sentences.
📏
Length & structure
4–16 bars works best. Mumble one idea per session — don't try to cram a whole song. Each pause you take becomes a new bar. Short, punchy phrases give tighter results than long run-ons.
🎛
Style controls
These directly change how the AI writes. Tone = how it hits. Mode = what it is (verse, hook, story). Vibe = the emotion behind it. Match all three to your idea before you generate.
🔁
Iterate
First result not perfect? Don't re-record — just switch up the style controls and hit Generate again on the same audio. Try Melodic vs Rap vs Punchy and pick what fits.
✅
Check the bars
Look at the Raw Transcription section after generating. It shows each bar with its syllable count. If a bar looks wrong (too many syllables or split badly), re-record that phrase more clearly.
QUICK COMBOS THAT WORK
Hook + Melodic + Sad = emotional chorus Verse + Aggressive + Hype = rap verse Hook + Simple + Love = singable R&B hook Story + Punchlines + Introspective = deep verse
